About Me

I am a web developer, with education and experience in full stack developing. My background in small business management gives me the skills to understand the importance of structure, problem solving, and planning.

Fueled by passion for learning, I consider myself a person who refuses to only know the minimal of any skill, or to complete any project without doing the best I can. I am willing to learn all aspects of any skill to perfect it to the best of my ability.

I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript in Lambda School. I then chose to attend University of Richmond's Full Stack coding bootcamp, where I increased my skills significantly, touching basis on skills including MySQL, MongoDB, Node.js, Express, MERN Stack, jQuery, Bootstrap, Heroku, Git, and Java.

I am excited to continue to develop my knowledge and gain experience as a web developer in Richmond, VA.
